Instructions

Follow these steps to create your customized football call-up image:

  1. Download the example player list and image template.
  2. Fill out the player list with the names and numbers of your players.
  3. Drag and drop the player names into the desired position in the web interface.
  4. Upload your filled-out list and your template image.
  5. Click "Generate Image" to receive your customized call-up image.
